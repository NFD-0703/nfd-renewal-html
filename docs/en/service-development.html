Core Capabilities

3 Core Development Services

We provide phased expertise from early-stage development through investment structure design.

Site Selection & Assessment

Leveraging our own development experience, we identify optimal IDC sites and conduct rigorous feasibility assessments of client-owned land.

  • Proprietary site selection and client land assessment
  • Practical analysis based on in-house development track record (Yongin Deokseong-ri IDC 80MW)
  • Risk and opportunity analysis by site condition
  • Comprehensive review of infrastructure, power, and regulatory environment

Project Due Diligence (DD)

We systematically execute Pre, Technical, Legal, and Tax DD to sharpen the accuracy of investment decision-making.

  • Pre DD · Technical DD · Legal DD · Tax DD execution
  • Application and review of proprietary Test Fit designs
  • Technical review reflecting target tenant requirements
  • Risk assessment reports to support investment decisions

Investment Structure & Business Model

Using the latest IDC market data and CAPEX benchmarks, we design optimal investment structures and business models tailored to each project.

  • Investment structure and business model design
  • Proprietary access to up-to-date IDC models and CAPEX data
  • High-precision feasibility analysis and financial modeling
  • Investor and financial institution presentation support

Service Process

Development Consulting Process

From initial site review to investment decision — we provide close support at every stage.

Step 01

Site Review & Selection

We analyze candidate sites against IDC suitability criteria and identify the optimal location for each client's requirements, with a comprehensive review of power, land use, and permitting environment.

Step 02

Due Diligence (DD) Execution

Starting with Pre DD, we conduct Technical, Legal, and Tax DD in sequence. Through proprietary Test Fit designs, we precisely calculate capacity and CAPEX to support confident decision-making.

Step 03

Investment Structure & Business Model Planning

Using the latest IDC market data, we structure investment frameworks and business models aligned with the client's objectives, enabling strategic decisions with confidence.
