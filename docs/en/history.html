History

From founding to today, a fast-moving track record of growth.

2026Q1-Q2

Acceleration of flagship developments

  • Architectural revision permit completed for Yongin Deokseong-ri IDC
  • Domestic customer acquisition in progress for Yongin Deokseong-ri IDC
  • Main construction launched with Samsung C&T for Yongin Deokseong-ri IDC
  • Phase 1 development launched for Dangjin Songsan IDC (200MW)
  • Phase 2 development launched for Dangjin Songsan IDC (100MW)
2025Q3-Q4

Expanded execution capability and brand integration

  • Renamed to NFD Korea through merger
  • Engineering and PM services delivered for Ansan IDC (40MW)
  • Global customer secured for Ansan IDC (40MW)
  • Earthwork construction began for Yongin Deokseong-ri IDC
  • Main construction contract executed for Yongin Deokseong-ri IDC
  • Development launched for Ansan Seonggok-dong IDC (40MW)
  • Structural enhancement consulting performed for Ansan Seonggok-dong IDC (40MW)
2025Q1-Q2

Partnership formation and pre-construction progress

  • Global CSP LOI received for Yongin Deokseong-ri IDC
  • Pre-construction launched with Samsung C&T for Yongin Deokseong-ri IDC
  • MOU signed with a Taiwan IDC developer to support customer acquisition
  • Development of NFD's proprietary liquid cooling design initiated
2024

Company launch and first major platform

  • NFD established in Seoul
  • Development launched for Yongin Deokseong-ri IDC (80MW)
  • Warburg Pincus secured as investor for Yongin Deokseong-ri IDC
