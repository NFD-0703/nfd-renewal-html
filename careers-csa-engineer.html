Senior CSA Engineer

Senior CSA engineer

Position Overview

We are seeking a Senior CSA Engineer with direct experience in Civil, Structural, and Architectural design for large-scale data center facilities. This role requires a strong background in design documentation, permitting, and coordination with MEP disciplines.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create and review Civil, Structural, and Architectural design for data center facilities
  • Create and manage CSA design documentation
  • Coordinate with MEP engineering disciplines for integrated design solutions
  • Manage technical reviews and documentation to ensure permitting compliance
  • Optimize designs for constructability, sustainability, and structural resilience
  • Conduct technical meetings and communicate effectively with international clients and partners in English

Required Experience and Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Architecture, Civil, or Structural Engineering
  • A minimum of 10 years of experience on large-scale infrastructure or mission-critical projects such as data centers
  • Proven experience in CSA design and documentation for industrial or commercial facilities
  • Proficiency with engineering tools (e.g., AutoCAD, Revit, Navisworks)
  • Strong English communication skills for technical discussions and client interaction
  • Candidates must have no restrictions on international business travel

Location

📍

Seoul headquarters or project sites in the Seoul metro area
Occasional overseas travel may be required.
