About NFD Korea

TRUSTED EXPERTS IN DATA CENTER INFRASTRUCTURE

NFD Korea (엔에프디코리아) is a specialized data center consulting and development company providing comprehensive services across the full lifecycle — from concept and feasibility through design, construction management, and commissioning.

What We Do

CORE SERVICES

From initial site assessment to full operational handover, NFD Korea provides integrated expertise across every stage of data center development.

01

Site Selection & Feasibility

Comprehensive site analysis including power availability, grid stability, fiber connectivity, environmental risk, and total cost of ownership modeling to identify the optimal location for your data center.

Site Analysis TCO Modeling Risk Assessment
02

Design & Engineering Advisory

Expert guidance through concept design, schematic design, and construction documentation — ensuring reliability, scalability, and energy efficiency are built in from the start.

Concept Design MEP Systems PUE Optimization
03

Project & Construction Management

End-to-end PMC services covering procurement strategy, contractor selection, schedule management, cost control, and quality assurance throughout the construction lifecycle.

PMC Cost Control QA/QC
04

Commissioning & Handover

Rigorous integrated systems testing (IST), commissioning, and operational readiness assessments to ensure your facility performs to specification from Day 1.

IST Commissioning ORT
05

Due Diligence & M&A Advisory

Technical due diligence for data center acquisitions, investments, and sale transactions — providing independent assessment of asset condition, capacity, compliance, and value.

Technical DD Valuation Support Risk Review
06

Operations & Optimization

Operational audits, maintenance strategy development, DCIM advisory, and energy efficiency improvement programs for existing data center facilities.

Ops Audit DCIM Energy Efficiency
