03 — Project Management

Phase-by-Phase Project
Management (PM) Services

Specialized PM professionals optimized for each project phase — development, design, construction, and operations.

Overview

Full-Lifecycle Data Center PM
Delivered by One Unified Team

NFD Korea directly executes PM services across the entire project lifecycle — from development through operations — within a single, unified organization. By maintaining consistent goals and strategy throughout every phase, we support the successful delivery of our clients' projects.

Our specialists in architecture, civil, electrical, mechanical, and controls engineering — with an average of 22 years' experience — take full accountability for meeting cost, quality, and schedule targets.

Core Strengths

3 Core PM Strengths

Discipline-specific expertise, integrated management systems, and proactive risk control — together, they deliver your project.

Discipline-Specific Expert Team

A PM team of seasoned, multi-year specialists leads your project from front to finish.

  • In-house experts across architecture, civil, electrical, mechanical, and controls
  • Average 22+ years of experience per discipline
  • Hands-on experience across all IDC development, design, and construction phases
  • Efficient management of all project stakeholders

Cost, Quality & Schedule Control

We operate an integrated management system to achieve our clients' cost, quality, and schedule targets without compromise.

  • Consistent goals and strategy maintained by a single unified organization
  • Proactive review of design changes (VE/VO) and cost impact analysis
  • Progress vs. plan monitoring with early corrective action
  • Owner-side quality inspection and acceptance framework

Proactive Risk Management

We identify and manage phase-specific risks in advance to keep the project on schedule and on target.

  • Phase-by-phase risk identification and mitigation planning
  • Integrated management of permitting, procurement, and construction risks
  • Structured stakeholder communication framework
  • Early operational readiness (OR) review and transition support
