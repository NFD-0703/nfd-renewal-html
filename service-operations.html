Core Services

3 Core Operations Services

Tenant-tailored leasing, operational efficiency, and service reliability — three pillars that define how we run your data center.

Tenant-Tailored Colocation

We provide colocation environments customized to diverse tenant profiles, with flexible SLA and contractual terms adapted to each tenant type.

  • Customized SLA and contract management by tenant type
  • Appropriate CAPEX alignment with development conditions
  • Tailored space configurations for hyperscalers, enterprise, and SME tenants
  • Tenant move-in readiness and onboarding support

High Operational Efficiency

We maximize facility management and operational efficiency to deliver cost-effective data center operations.

  • Maximized facility management and operational efficiency
  • Continuous infrastructure capacity management to sustain efficiency
  • Energy management to achieve PUE targets

Operational Management Capabilities

Facility Management

Integrated Facility Management

We operate an integrated management system covering electrical, mechanical, fire safety, security, and communications infrastructure. Dedicated specialists monitor facility status in real time and carry out maintenance across all disciplines.

Capacity Management

Infrastructure Capacity Management

We continuously analyze power, cooling, and space capacity to proactively respond to shifts in tenant demand. Through disciplined capacity planning and optimization, we sustain operational efficiency at all times.

Tenant Interface

Tenant Interface Management

Through dedicated points of contact for each tenant, we handle SLA compliance reporting, infrastructure change requests, and issue resolution with speed and accountability. Tenant satisfaction is managed as a core KPI.
